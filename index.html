About Me

I started coding academically in Summer 2020. I have always had a passion for video games and especially creating them. My first introduction to game development was playing RPG Maker II on the PS2. This sparked a passion in me that for a long time I resisted. I grew up being told video games are not a career. I tired several education and career paths including Music Education, Chemistry and I even sold cars for 6 weeks at a local Nissan dealership. All these experience made one thing clear to me. I want to make games. In 2022 My New Year Resolution was to put my nose to the grind stone and FINISH some games. I accomplished this in February when I took part in the Ludam Dare 50 game jam and from that my first publicly available game "Escape The Pipes" was born. This lit a fire under me and motivated me to compete in the 2022 UNR ACM Hackathon. This is where everything changed for me. My team and I took 1st place! My future goals are to finish my degree, create a commercially viable indie game, get into a game studio, get my name in the credits my first major project. I am motivated, I am hungry, and nothing will get in the way of me and my goals.
